Granja Paraíso 92 is Wilton Benitez's flagship farm in Piendamó, a few kilometers from Diego Bermudez's Finca El Paraíso. The property holds an unusual collection of varieties for Colombia — Sudan Rume, Eugenoides, Java, Wush Wush, Pink Bourbon, Chiroso, alongside the usual Castillo — planted in small experimental blocks. Processing is done on-site in a UV- and ozone-sterilized environment that Benitez describes more like a lab than a beneficio. Sister farms La Macarena and Las Brisas, which Benitez also runs, are smaller experimental plots; their lots are sometimes blended with Granja Paraíso 92's, sometimes sold separately.

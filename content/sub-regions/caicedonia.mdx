Caicedonia is a mid-sized municipality at the northern tip of Valle del Cauca, on the Quindío border. Café Granja La Esperanza and its sister farms have been the most-recognized producers here since the 1990s. Altitudes 1,400–1,800 m; primarily Caturra and Castillo with notable Gesha plantings.

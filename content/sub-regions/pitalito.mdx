Pitalito is Huila's main coffee town, a market hub for the Macizo Colombiano and the surrounding farms. Its volume is what makes Huila a top-producing department; in recent years the area has also become a quiet center for microlot experimentation, particularly with naturals and anaerobic processing on Caturra and Pink Bourbon.

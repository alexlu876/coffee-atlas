Piendamó is the small Cauca municipality where the Bermudez and Benitez farms sit, roughly 30 km north of Popayán. The combination of a stable 18–22°C average, granitic soil, and proximity to research-oriented producers has made it the most-watched square mile in specialty coffee since around 2018.

Armenia is the capital of Quindío and the operational center of the Eje Cafetero. The Arcila family farms — Esmeralda, Villarazo, Santa Mónica, Maracay, Buenos Aires — sit in the surrounding municipalities. The town has been a seat of Colombia's coffee industry administration for decades; nearby Calarcá and Circasia are smaller adjacent producing zones.

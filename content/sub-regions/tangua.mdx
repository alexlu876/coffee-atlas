Tangua is a small municipality southwest of Pasto in Nariño, on the western flank of the Galeras volcano. Pablo Guerrero's Finca El Obraje is the best-known farm here. High altitude (1,800–2,200 m) and steep volcanic slopes; mostly Caturra and Castillo with a small amount of Gesha.
